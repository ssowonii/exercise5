Exercise 5: CSS Layout

This week you'll be exploring CSS Layout, which as per this week's lecture deck boils it down to three principals: the box model, floating, and positioning. Below, we're going to take a pass at them on their own—and in combination—to get a sense of the fundamentals. These are all a little tricky, and there is no 'right' way to solve them, there are several ways to get the results that have been asked for. Experiment and pay close attention, the 'floating' example, in particular, is the key to parcelling up a page with HTML/CSS.
